Google Workspace FAQ

Answers to common questions about Google Workspace, setup, billing, and features.

Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) is a collection of cloud-based productivity and collaboration tools developed by Google. It comes with a custom email for your business. It's everything you need to get anything done, now in one place — including Gmail, Drive, Meet, Calendar, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Forms, Sites, and more.

G Suite has been rebranded to Google Workspace to reflect the platform's vision to offer you an integrated experience across multiple communication and collaboration tools. However, most of G Suite's functionalities and programs remain the same — with additional features and improvements continuously added.

You will get a Gmail connected with your business email address and other familiar tools — Calendar, Meet, Chat, Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Forms, Sites, and more — all built with trusted Google security and best-in-class search technology.

Choose the proposed package at our pricing table above. After reaching out via WhatsApp or our contact form, our team at BlacTec will set up your account, connect your domain, and have you running within hours. We handle all the technical setup so you can focus on your business.

Whether you are just starting your own business or already running a small enterprise, Google Workspace is ready to fit your needs, including security tools, compliance features, and multiple cloud storage options. It's ideal when you need professional email, team collaboration, video conferencing, or cloud file management.

We offer Google Workspace packages starting from Business Starter with 30GB storage per mailbox, antivirus feature and advanced malware protection — all the way to Business Plus with 5TB storage and advanced compliance tools.
